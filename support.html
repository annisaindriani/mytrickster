GAME SUPPORT INFORMATION

MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

  • Operating System (OS): Windows 10 64bits
  • CPU (Processor): Celeron G530 2.4Ghz or similar
  • GPU (Graphics): Nvidia GT 650M or similar

RECOMMENDED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

  • Operating System (OS): Windows 10 (64-bit)s
  • CPU (Processor): Intel i3-6100T 3.2Ghz or similar
  • GPU (Graphics): Nvidia GTX 970 or similar

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • RAM (Memory): Minimum 4 GB
  • Storage: Minimum 5 GB available space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection
  • DirectX: Version 11

NOTES

  1. Ensure that your graphics drivers are up to date to avoid performance issues.
  2. For an enhanced gaming experience, a solid-state drive (SSD) is recommended.
  3. Close unnecessary background applications to optimize system resources while playing.
  4. Keep an eye on the official website or game launcher for any updates or announcements regarding system requirements.

If you encounter any technical issues or have questions about your system's compatibility, feel free to contact our Support Team for assistance. Our team is dedicated to providing you with the best gaming experience.

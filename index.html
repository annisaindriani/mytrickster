"Chronicles of Taming: A Creatures Odyssey"

In the heart of the Airborne Archipelago, where the sky meets the sea and adventure beckons, a young tamer named Luna embarks on a journey that transcends the boundaries of time. Luna, armed with determination and a dream to become the Tamer of Harmony, sets foot on the vibrant island of Deniz.

Guided by the wind and the whispers of ancient prophecies, Luna befriends a mischievous Tuwai and a stalwart Saipat. Together, they weave a tale of friendship, challenges, and the unbreakable bond between humans and creatures.

"Chronicles of Taming: A Creatures Odyssey" is not just a journey through a virtual realm; it's an exploration of friendship, courage, and the enduring spirit of adventure. Join Luna as she discovers the magic of creatures and the power of unity in a world where every step is a leap into the unknown.

MAPS

Deniz Island
Deniz (”Mare Nostrum”) is a Mediterranean paradise of
glittering seas where Water and Wind Creatures abound.
Walk the streets of Turquesa, explore the ruins of an
ancient fort or just chill.

Throughout the Archipelago, Deniz has the reputation of a
beautiful island with amazing beaches, historic castles,
and majestic waters.

Omninesia Island
Omninesia (”the Myrisles”) is a cluster of tropical islets
connected by rope bridges, home of Nature and Fire
Creatures.

Omninesians create all kinds of foods and products from
natural resources. Omninesia also has a long tradition of
wind and percussion instruments, possibly influenced by its
population of Wind types.

Tucma Island
Tucma, “The End of All Things” - a vast desolation of ashes,
noxious lakes and Toxic Creatures. Under the surface, the
mining city of Quetzal is home to artisans, rock-hoppers
and Crystal Creatures.

A gas-mask is strongly recommended for the exploration
of the surface of Tucma, as is avoiding the eastern
Corrupted Badlands.

Arbury Island
Arbury (”The Alma Mater”) is an island in MyTrickStEr. Its
pride is its University. The fierce dons of Lochburg swear by
their Melee Creatures, against the sophisticated Properton
academics, who favour Mental Creatures.

Arbury has traditionally been considered a melancholy land
of constant rain, rolling green hills and endless academic
quibbles.

TOWNS

Brical de Mar

Brical de Mar is the first town on Deniz, where
tamers begin their journey. It's a small coastal town
with essential facilities like Temporium (item shop)
and the Tamer's School.

Mokupuni

Mokupuni is the main town on Omninesia, known
for its desert aesthetics and research centers.
Tamers can find Temporiums and explore the local
culture.

GAME TOWN

Mokupuni

Description

Mokupuni is the main town on Omninesia, known for its desert aesthetics and research centers. Tamers can find Temporiums and explore the local culture. Mokupuni is the twin town of Nanga and located in Omninesia. It is described as the fruit capital of the Myrisles, and still has all the flavor of traditional Omninesia.

Items

  • Pillow (Reward from Paparazzo quest)
  • TC004: Wake Up (Reward from Homework quest)

Trivia

  • "Feleti And the Jar of Cookies" is a traditional Mokupuni fable featuring a Hidody as the protagonist.
  • Mokupuni is Hawaiian for "island".
